Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like to delete your data associated with our app, please follow the instructions below:

  1. Send an email to support@yourdomain.com with the subject: "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include the following information in your email:
    • Your name
    • Your email address used in the app
    • Any other details to help us locate your account
  3. We will respond within 7 business days to confirm your data has been deleted.

For more information, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
